Paris, France


Paris, the capital of France, is known for its rich history, stunning architecture, and world-class cuisine. Often referred to as the "City of Light," it is a dream destination for travelers who enjoy art, culture, and romance.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – The iconic symbol of Paris and a must-visit landmark.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to the famous Mona Lisa and countless art treasures.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A masterpiece of Gothic architecture.
  4. Champs-Élysées – A world-famous avenue for shopping and dining.
  5. Seine River Cruise – A scenic way to experience the beauty of Paris.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissants – Flaky, buttery pastries perfect for breakfast.
  • Macarons – Colorful and delicious French delicacies.
  • Baguettes – Crisp on the outside, soft on the inside.
  • Escargots – A classic French delicacy, often served with garlic butter.
  • Crème Brûlée – A creamy dessert with a caramelized sugar crust.
